# Jonas Ghebrezgiabiher

<img src="https://cdn.runevents.net/speaker-profile-images/4b50c0cf-49d6-4def-961e-43b8cd7108b5" alt="Jonas Ghebrezgiabiher" style="max-width:300px; max-height:300px; width:auto; height:auto;" />

Jonas is an accomplished Senior IT Project Manager and Trainer at Mercedes-Benz Tech Innovation, specializing in Azure and Power Platform technologies. As a Microsoft Certified Trainer (MCT), Jonas holds an impressive array of certifications, including:
•	Microsoft Certified Fabric Analytics Engineer Associate
•	Microsoft Certified Azure Enterprise-Scale Data Analyst Associate
•	Microsoft Certified Azure Data Engineer Associate
•	Microsoft Certified Power BI Data Analyst Associate
Jonas has successfully led IT projects at Mercedes-Benz, driving and promoting innovation within the organization. His expertise in Azure and Power Platform has been instrumental in transforming data management and analytics processes.
In addition to his role at Mercedes-Benz, Jonas is dedicated to constantly educating others about technologies through his work as an MCT. Over the years he has provided numerous training curriculums and delivered successful training sessions across Germany, helping fellow professionals enhance their skills and knowledge. Jonas is also a member of the MCT Community in Germany, where he shares his expertise and passion for Microsoft technology.
