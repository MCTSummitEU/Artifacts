# Creating a positive & engaging learning environment for MCTs

**Speaker(s):** Jonas Ghebrezgiabiher — session on the [European MCT Summit 2025, Manchester](https://e.runevents.net/european-mct-summit-2025) agenda.

## Abstract

Creating a positive and engaging learning environment is essential for effective training, but it also comes with its challenges. This session is designed to equip Microsoft Certified Trainers (MCTs) with the skills to foster strong connections with their learners while managing challenging situations.

Session Objectives:
•	Understanding the Importance of Rapport: Learn why building rapport is crucial for learner engagement and success.
•	Techniques for Building Rapport: Discover a variety of techniques to establish and maintain a strong connection with your learners, including active listening, empathy, and personalized interactions.
•	Creating an Inclusive Environment: Explore methods to ensure all learners feel valued and included, promoting a sense of community and collaboration.
•	Identifying Difficult Situations: Recognize common challenging behaviors and situations that can arise in the classroom. Gain practical strategies for addressing and diffusing difficult situations including the Harvard concept and turn disruptions into learning opportunities.

Why Attend: 
This session is for all MCTs, whether you are new to training or a seasoned professional looking to enhance your interpersonal and classroom management skills. By focusing on both building rapport and handling difficult situations, this session will help you to create a more engaging, supportive, and effective learning environment.
