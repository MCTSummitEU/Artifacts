# Tools and Apps to Super Power Your Training

**Speaker(s):** Sara Fennah, Tiago Costa, Julian Sharp — session on the [European MCT Summit 2025, Manchester](https://e.runevents.net/european-mct-summit-2025) agenda.

## Abstract

This "unconference" session will give you the opportunity to learn about tools and apps to take you training to the next level.  Considering tools such as quizzing and whiteboarding apps to engage the learners and strategies for different delivery modalities, the session team will guide you through this discussion giving you the opportunity to share and contribute as well as learn.
