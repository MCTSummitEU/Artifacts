# Gareth Jones

<img src="https://cdn.runevents.net/speaker-profile-images/e5dd70c5-9b25-4394-8d97-772f7c049c4f" alt="Gareth Jones" style="max-width:300px; max-height:300px; width:auto; height:auto;" />

Gareth is a Microsoft Certified Trainer, a UK Regional Lead and a Microsoft MVP in Azure. He has spoken at multiple Microsoft events worldwide on a plethora of subjects but now specialises in Azure and System Center. He has been working in IT since the NT 4 days and served in the British Army for 11 years where he worked in IT and Communications deploying to Bosnia, Macedonia, Afghanistan and Iraq. When he isn’t teaching he consults on the Microsoft cloud services to multiple clients around the world.  In his spare time he enjoys skiing, travelling and keeping up with the ever changing IT space.
