# I'm an introvert... Can I stand in front of a classroom?

**Speaker(s):** Rex de Koning — session on the [European MCT Summit 2025, Manchester](https://e.runevents.net/european-mct-summit-2025) agenda.

## Abstract

We've all been there, we see all those superstar MCT's in front of a class room delivering trainings, talking about all kinds of things... And we think they are all so extroverted as can be... What if I tell you that is not the case for a lot of them, and they are actually introverts....

But how do you get to stand in front of a classroom or audience telling a story or teaching a class?

First of all, the right mindset, perseverence and.... Join my session to hear my story about this
