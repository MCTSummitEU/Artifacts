# Riding the Tech Wave: Expanding Your Training Portfolio with CompTIA Certifications

**Speaker(s):** Zeshan Sattar — session on the [European MCT Summit 2025, Manchester](https://e.runevents.net/european-mct-summit-2025) agenda.

## Abstract

In the rapidly evolving world of technology, staying ahead of the curve is essential for any MCT. As tech trends and regulations like NIS2 reshape the industry landscape, the demand for comprehensive and versatile training solutions is at an all-time high. This session will empower MCTs to expand their training portfolio by incorporating CompTIA certification-aligned courses, thereby enhancing their marketability and utilisation.

Join us to explore the synergies between Microsoft and CompTIA certifications and discover how you can leverage these opportunities to meet the growing needs of your clients. We'll delve into the latest CompTIA certifications, including those in cybersecurity, networking, and cloud computing, and discuss how they align with current tech trends, regulatory requirements, and global skills frameworks such as NICE, DoD 8140, and the ECSF.

By the end of this session, you'll be equipped with the knowledge and inspiration to diversify your offerings, attract a broader audience, and position yourself as a leader in the tech training industry. Don't miss this chance to ride the tech wave and future-proof your career as an MCT!
