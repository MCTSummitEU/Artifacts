# Zeshan Sattar

<img src="https://cdn.runevents.net/speaker-profile-images/7db04354-0aac-4a4f-9f12-a85db47e0ecd" alt="Zeshan Sattar" style="max-width:300px; max-height:300px; width:auto; height:auto;" />

Zeshan Sattar is the Senior Director of Industry Relations EMEA at CompTIA, the leading provider of vendor-neutral IT certifications in the world. With over 15 years of experience in the digital and IT industries, Zeshan designs and delivers certification-driven programmes for different audiences, including apprenticeships, academic and professional learning. His current focus is on cyber security, AI at work, cloud computing, workforce development, and programme management.
