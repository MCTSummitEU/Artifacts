# Cindy Haast

With over 20 years of experience in senior marketing and business development roles, Cindy transitioned into IT and Enterprise Architecture to empower clients with the knowledge and tools needed to optimise their business operations. As a TOGAF 10 Certified Enterprise Architect and ITIL4 Strategist and Managing Professional, Cindy is dedicated to providing strategic guidance and effective solutions to cultivate and grow her client's businesses.

With over six years experience As a Microsoft Certified Trainer, Cindy delivers both onsite, hybrid and remote training, leveraging her deep expertise and enthusiasm for Microsoft technologies. Her goal is to equip clients with the skills and insights they need to fully utilise Dynamics 365 and Microsoft Power Platform, ultimately enhancing their operational efficiency and achieving their strategic objectives.
