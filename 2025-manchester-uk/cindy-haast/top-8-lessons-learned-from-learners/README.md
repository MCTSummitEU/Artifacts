# Top 8 Lessons Learned from Learners

**Speaker(s):** Cindy Haast — session on the [European MCT Summit 2025, Manchester](https://e.runevents.net/european-mct-summit-2025) agenda.

## Abstract

Problem Statement/Context:
In today's fast-paced, technology-driven world, traditional training methods often fail to engage learners effectively, leading to frustration and information overload. Many learners, especially from Generation Y and Z, struggle with long training sessions, inadequate prerequisite knowledge, and complex exam scenarios that seem disconnected from real-world applications. Additionally, the constant evolution of technology demands a more agile approach to training, which traditional methods do not address. This session explores these challenges and presents practical strategies to make technical training more effective, engaging, and relevant.

Session Topics:
This session will delve into the common challenges faced by learners in technical training environments, such as attention span limitations, the impact of insufficient prerequisite knowledge, and the misalignment between course content and exam requirements. We will discuss the drawbacks of traditional training formats, which can disrupt work-life balance and lead to dissatisfaction among clients and supervisors. The session will also explore the benefits of using storytelling, graphical representations, and hands-on activities to avoid information overload. Furthermore, we will examine the pros and cons of online versus in-person training, and the importance of keeping up with rapidly evolving technology through continuous learning approaches.

Key Takeaways:
Attendees will leave with actionable insights and techniques to enhance their classroom management and training delivery style. They will learn how to structure training sessions that align with modern attention spans, ensure learners have the necessary prerequisite knowledge, and prepare students for real-world exam scenarios. Participants will also gain an understanding of how to reduce the impact of training on day-to-day work through flexible scheduling. Additionally, they will learn how to create engaging, immersive learning experiences that cater to both online and in-person learners. Finally, attendees will discover the importance of adopting a continuous learning approach to stay current with technological advancements.

Products and Technologies:
This session will focus on Microsoft training methodologies and technologies, particularly those related to Microsoft Certified Trainer programs. Examples will be given in the context of Microsoft Power Platform, Microsoft Dynamics 365, Microsoft Azure and Microsoft 365 and AI courses. The session will also cover tools and platforms used for delivering both online and in-person training, ensuring a hybrid approach that meets diverse learner needs.
