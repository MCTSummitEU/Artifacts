# Transforming Training with AI

**Speaker(s):** Tiago Costa — session on the [European MCT Summit 2025, Manchester](https://e.runevents.net/european-mct-summit-2025) agenda.

## Abstract

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is revolutionizing the world, and the training landscape is no exception. AI can offer innovative ways to enhance learning experiences, personalize content, and increase engagement. 
This session is not just about theory; it's about practical applications. We'll explore how MCTs can leverage AI to transform their training programs, using AI-powered assessment tools, chatbots for learner support and integrating AI features in Microsoft tools such as Microsoft 365 and Teams.
Participants will learn how to use AI for learning analytics, enabling them to gain deep insights into learner behavior and performance. We'll also cover how AI can create interactive and gamified learning experiences, making training sessions more engaging and effective.
Furthermore, we'll discuss the ethical considerations of using AI in training, ensuring data privacy, and addressing potential biases in AI algorithms. 
Finally, we will explore future trends in AI, such as Generative AI and natural language processing (NLP), preparing trainers for the next wave of technological advancements in education. 
Join me on the journey to discover how AI can elevate your training programs and stay ahead in the evolving field of education.
