# Scaling Lab Preparations for Microsoft Training Delivery: Navigating MFA, Tenant Management, and more

**Speaker(s):** Amit Malik — session on the [European MCT Summit 2025, Manchester](https://e.runevents.net/european-mct-summit-2025) agenda.

## Abstract

In today's Microsoft training environment, preparing labs for delivery has become increasingly complex due to evolving requirements such as MFA enforcement on key portals (Azure, Intune, etc.), stockpile tenant unavailability from from Microsoft, need for dedicated Entra ID tenants and managing quota expectations. In this session, we will dive deep into how our team successfully enables labs for over 500+ official and custom workshops every month, overcoming these challenges at scale. Attendees will learn best practices for managing tenants, streamlining lab preparation processes, and handling the technical hurdles of providing seamless learning experiences. We'll share key lessons learned, from troubleshooting tenant issues to automating MFA workarounds and delivering consistent quality for both trainers and participants. Whether you're delivering workshops for small groups or thousands of learners, this session will equip you with actionable strategies for efficient lab management and delivery.
