# Remote and Hybrid IT Training: Practical Teaching Strategies and Tips for MCTs

**Speaker(s):** Jonah Andersson — session on the [European MCT Summit 2025, Manchester](https://e.runevents.net/european-mct-summit-2025) agenda.

## Abstract

Since the pandemic, remote working and hybrid training for MCTs have became the new normal for us. It has its pros and cons and its challenges. It is not like the in-person training classroom environment. In this this session, you will learn about the practical list of strategies and tips to improve your remote training with your learners . Explore the essential tools for virtual remote training classrooms regardless of the barriers of location. Learn how using the collaborative, technology, and AI tools we have today can help in creating engaging lessons in your virtual classrooms while keeping the interests for learning fun, inspiring and have a positive collaborative learning community.
