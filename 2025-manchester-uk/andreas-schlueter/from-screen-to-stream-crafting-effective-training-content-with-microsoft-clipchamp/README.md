# From Screen to Stream: Crafting Effective Training Content with Microsoft Clipchamp

**Speaker(s):** Andreas Schlüter — session on the [European MCT Summit 2025, Manchester](https://e.runevents.net/european-mct-summit-2025) agenda.

## Abstract

Join Andreas Schlüter for an engaging session on Microsoft Clipchamp.  
In this session, Andreas will demonstrate the various features of Clipchamp that can help trainers create their own training content. Participants will learn how to create screen recordings, webcasts, GIFs, and more. He will also cover the editing tools available in Clipchamp, such as cutting and merging videos, adding text and transitions, and using effects and filters. Additionally, attendees will explore the different export options to share their finished videos on various platforms. This session aims to empower trainers with the knowledge and skills to enhance their training sessions with dynamic and interactive content. Last but not least, Andreas will talk about his three top tipps for Microsoft Clipchamp.
