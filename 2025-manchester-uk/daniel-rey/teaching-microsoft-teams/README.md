# Teaching Microsoft Teams

**Speaker(s):** Daniel Rey — session on the [European MCT Summit 2025, Manchester](https://e.runevents.net/european-mct-summit-2025) agenda.

## Abstract

Have you ever struggled to come up with additional content, discussions, materials, supplemental exercises, etc. for the four day MS-700 course? Here are some ideas of ways to stretch the four modules (which used to be six) into related topics, hands on labs, discussions, demos and other activities to keep your group engaged for four full days. Come learn some things that aren’t well documented that you can add as supplementary material. Share anything you have added as an exercise, handout, analogy or discussion for others. This will be highly interactive and focused around the MS-700 Managing Microsoft Teams course
