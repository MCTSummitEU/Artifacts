# So you've become an MCT - What Now?

**Speaker(s):** Joe Griffin — session on the [European MCT Summit 2025, Manchester](https://e.runevents.net/european-mct-summit-2025) agenda.

## Abstract

Congratulations on becoming an MCT. Now, what do you do? There's a link over here for some benefits. Oh, and something else about the MCT Cafe. Where can I find the courseware? And how do I get hold of that nice shiny polo shirt I see other trainers wear? This is all so confusing!

Sound familiar? If you have recently become an MCT and are still finding your feet through what's available and the various benefits available, this is the session for you. As well as covering the variety of goodies available directly from Microsoft, we'll also review the opportunities to access various lab vendor platforms and the steps involved as part of this. With plenty of opportunity to ask questions, the session aims to be an informative and "safe" space to ask any question - remember, there's no such thing as a stupid question ;)
