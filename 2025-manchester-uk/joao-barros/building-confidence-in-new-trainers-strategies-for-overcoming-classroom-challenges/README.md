# Building Confidence in New Trainers: Strategies for Overcoming Classroom Challenges

**Speaker(s):** Joao Barros — session on the [European MCT Summit 2025, Manchester](https://e.runevents.net/european-mct-summit-2025) agenda.

## Abstract

New trainers often face a range of challenges that can impact their confidence and classroom effectiveness. This session is designed to empower new MCTs with practical strategies to navigate common classroom obstacles and enhance their delivery skills. We will cover key topics such as handling difficult student behaviors, managing unexpected technical issues, and creating a positive learning environment. By the end of this session, participants will have a toolkit of proven techniques to build their confidence and establish themselves as competent trainers in any learning environment.
