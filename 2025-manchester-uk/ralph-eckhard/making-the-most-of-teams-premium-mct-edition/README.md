# Making the most of Teams Premium - MCT Edition

**Speaker(s):** Ralph Eckhard — session on the [European MCT Summit 2025, Manchester](https://e.runevents.net/european-mct-summit-2025) agenda.

## Abstract

Teams Premium has some great features for improving meetings. Templates, labeling... And much more. Many of these features can benefit MCT's in delivering online training. We'll have a look at the different features and how you can implement them for your online sessions!
