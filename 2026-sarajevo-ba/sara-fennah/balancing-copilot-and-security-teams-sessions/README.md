# Balancing Copilot and Security in Teams Sessions: Tips for MCTs

**Speaker(s):** Sara Fennah — session on the [MCT EU Summit 2026](https://e.runevents.net/mct-eu-summit-2026) agenda.

## Abstract

As Microsoft Certified Trainers, we’re constantly adapting to new tools that enhance learning experiences. Copilot in Microsoft Teams is the 'Hero' Copilot feature in Microsoft 365, but it also introduces unique challenges for trainers. Many MCTs deliver sessions as guests or external users, limiting access to and control of Copilot. Transcription and recording also raises concerns around intellectual property protection and safeguarding participant data.

In this session, I’ll share practical strategies tailored for MCTs to get the most from Copilot in Teams, without compromising on security. We’ll explore:
• Built-in security features that support safe learning environments, including lobby controls, meeting roles, and secure guest access
• Advanced protections available with Teams Premium, such as watermarking, end-to-end encryption, and sensitivity labels
• Simple, effective ways to balance AI-driven productivity with robust data protection

Whether you’re training on Copilot or using it to streamline delivery, you’ll leave with actionable steps to help you stay productive, professional, and protected.

Key Takeaways:
• Understand the key security considerations for MCTs using AI in Teams sessions
• Learn which features help protect learner data—without disabling Copilot
• Gain practical tips to apply immediately in your training delivery
