# Escaping the Digital Past

**Speaker(s):** Nedim Sadovic — session on the [MCT EU Summit 2026](https://e.runevents.net/mct-eu-summit-2026) agenda.

## Abstract

Many organizations still rely on legacy technologies, unaware of how deeply they limit efficiency, security, and long‑term scalability. Active Directory, NTFS, Group Policy, WSUS, and older Windows Server versions once formed the backbone of IT operations — but today’s environments demand faster, more secure, and more adaptable platforms.
This session exposes the hidden risks of staying anchored in outdated systems and highlights modern alternatives that deliver agility, resilience, and operational clarity. Attendees will learn how to transition from legacy infrastructure to cloud‑ready, policy‑driven, and automation‑focused solutions without disrupting business continuity. Through practical guidance and real‑world patterns, we demonstrate how stepping out of the digital past unlocks a more secure and future‑proof IT landscape.
