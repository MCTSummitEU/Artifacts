# How to Deliver Sessions People Actually Remember (11 Years of MCT Lessons)

**Speaker(s):** Robert Atkinson — session on the [MCT EU Summit 2026](https://e.runevents.net/mct-eu-summit-2026) agenda.

## Abstract

Most people think great speakers are born confident. They’re not.
In this session, I’ll share my journey from being the scared kid who didn’t feel like they belonged, to delivering my first session and building 11 years of experience as a Microsoft Certified Trainer. This isn’t a “perfect story” talk. It’s the real version: nerves, self-doubt, learning the hard way, and slowly building confidence through repetition and resilience.

You’ll learn what actually makes sessions engaging (even if you’re not naturally outgoing), how to keep attention in the first 60 seconds, and the small changes that transform “just a presentation” into an experience people remember.

Whether you’re new to speaking, new to training, or just want to level up your delivery, this will give you practical tools you can use immediately.
