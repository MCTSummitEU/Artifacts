# Shaping the MCT of the future (Panel)

**Speaker(s):** Rolf McLaughlin, Sara Fennah, Amelia Hernández Osorio, Ronald Beekelaar — session on the [MCT EU Summit 2026](https://e.runevents.net/mct-eu-summit-2026) agenda.

So you have been a MCT for many years. Years of honing your skills and always learning all by yourself. But what  about others and how they are preparing for the future? Or how do you make sure your teaching skills and delivery methods are on par with what the market needs? Let's talk!
