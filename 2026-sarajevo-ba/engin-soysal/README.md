I'm Engin Soysal, a Microsoft MVP and Microsoft Certified Trainer with over 15 years of hands-on experience in Microsoft 365, Azure, and modern workplace architecture.

I specialize in designing, migrating, and securing real-world Microsoft environments, with a strong focus on simplicity, scalability, and security. My work spans Microsoft 365, Azure, Intune, identity, and automation, always grounded in practical implementation rather than theory.

As a speaker, I focus on real lessons learned from production environments: architectural choices, trade-offs, and the things Microsoft doesn't always highlight in the documentation. My goal is to help others avoid common pitfalls and make better design decisions.

I'm passionate about sharing knowledge, building community, and helping professionals get the most out of the Microsoft ecosystem through clear, honest, and experience-driven sessions.
