# Omar Kudovic

<img src="https://cdn.runevents.net/speaker-profile-images/616dabd4-6dfa-49a3-a1a6-e1e8a8b6803d" alt="Omar Kudovic" style="max-width:300px; max-height:300px; width:auto; height:auto;" />

With over 15 years of experience primarily focused on Cloud solutions, Security & Compliance, and integration of Cloud services and applications within business enterprise environments, I bring a wealth of expertise. For the past decade, I have dedicated my efforts to enhancing security and compliance measures, particularly within Microsoft365 solutions.

I've had the privilege of sharing my knowledge and insights with various audiences, including the Microsoft Community of Bosnia and Herzegovina and other major conferences, such as the MS Community Conference, MS Network Conference, and European Cloud Summit, European Collaboration Summit, Microsoft Indeed, M365 Conference Chicago among others. My expertise extends to diverse areas such as cybversecurity, compliance, endpoint protection, audio, video, voice, and messaging solutions.

In addition to my professional orientation, I actively contribute to the Microsoft community as a leader in Bosnia and Herzegovina, where I both speak and organize meetings and conferences on the integration of Cloud solutions, Microsoft365, Azure, and Security domains. My recent focus has been on Microsoft365 Security and Compliance, reflecting my commitment to staying at the forefront of cutting-edge technologies and industry best practices. Co-author of the Packt Publishing book: "Microsoft 365 Security and Compliance for Administrators: A definitive guide to planning, implementing, and maintaining Microsoft 365 security posture", published 2024. Last 13 years awarded with Microsoft MVP award.

Private: Hi-Fi Audiophile, Rockabilly culture and music, wine, music records collector.
