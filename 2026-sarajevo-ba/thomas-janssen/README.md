# Thomas Janssen

Over the past ten years, Thomas Janssen has helped organizations work more efficiently by leveraging emerging technologies. His expertise has not gone unnoticed: Microsoft recognized him as a Microsoft Certified Trainer, acknowledging his deep technical knowledge and ability to translate innovation into real business value.

Delivering more than one hundred trainings each year and running a YouTube channel visited by over one million people annually, Thomas shares his knowledge and passion for artificial intelligence and automation with a global audience.
