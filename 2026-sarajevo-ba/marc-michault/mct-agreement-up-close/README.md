# The MCT Agreement, up close and personal

**Speaker(s):** Marc Michault — session on the [MCT EU Summit 2026](https://e.runevents.net/mct-eu-summit-2026) agenda.

## Abstract

All Microsoft Certified Trainers commit to abiding by the rules set out in the MCT Agreement (and other agreements with Microsoft, just to make it interesting). So... have you read it? Did you know it was changed in the last year? Want to discuss what it means to you?

We will review the main point of that agreement and others tied to it, the risks associated with them, what we are allowed to do and not, and take an historical perspective of it.
