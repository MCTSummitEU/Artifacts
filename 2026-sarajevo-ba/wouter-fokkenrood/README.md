# Wouter Fokkenrood

<img src="https://cdn.runevents.net/speaker-profile-images/e2456d52-9d85-4d53-9331-1aeb4b93b2a2" alt="Wouter Fokkenrood" style="max-width:300px; max-height:300px; width:auto; height:auto;" />

Wouter Fokkenrood is an experienced Consultant and Trainer with a strong focus on making complex technical subjects clear, engaging, and memorable.

Over the years, Wouter has delivered numerous trainings and sessions within the Microsoft ecosystem. Throughout his work, he discovered that successful learning is not just about what you teach, but how you tell the story. Facts, slides, and demos become far more impactful when they are wrapped in a clear, relatable narrative.

In his sessions, Wouter helps trainers move beyond content delivery and develop storytelling as a core training skill. His approach is practical, energetic, and grounded in real‑world training experience, enabling trainers to better connect with their audience and improve knowledge retention.

Wouter combines technical expertise with a passion for communication, helping trainers turn information into experiences people actually remember
