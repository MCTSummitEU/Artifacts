# Teaching with Copilot – Microsoft 365 & Copilot Studio

**Speaker(s):** Frank Falvey — session on the [MCT EU Summit 2026](https://e.runevents.net/mct-eu-summit-2026) agenda.

## Abstract

Teaching with Copilot – Microsoft 365 & Copilot Studio is a hands-on session designed to help end users confidently and responsibly use Microsoft 365 Copilot in their daily work. Participants learn how to collaborate with Copilot across Outlook, Teams, Word, Excel, and PowerPoint, develop effective prompting skills, and understand how Copilot works within organisational data, security, and governance boundaries. The session also introduces Copilot Studio, showing how custom, role-based copilots can support business processes and knowledge sharing. The session focuses on practical scenarios, AI literacy, and real-world adoption ensuring Copilot delivers lasting productivity gains.
