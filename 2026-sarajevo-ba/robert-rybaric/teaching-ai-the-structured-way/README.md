# Teaching AI the structured way

**Speaker(s):** Robert Rybaric — session on the [MCT EU Summit 2026](https://e.runevents.net/mct-eu-summit-2026) agenda.

## Abstract

Do you also struggle to absorb the explosively developing area of AI? Would you like to have a strucured approach how to deliver the big picture to your learners? Do you also see, that there is neither a MOC course, nor a single Microsoft Learn landing page, which goves you the starting point? You are not alone. In this session I will present you a structured way, how you can start to deliver the AI know-how to your learnes, so that they do not get lost, rather understand all the puzzle pieces and how they play together. I will show you, how you can give your learners in one single session the understanding of Copilots, Agents, Agentic AI, Azure AI, tools used to build AI solutions etc. This approach will represent you hub, form which you can go in either direction and teach einther some specialized MOC courses or you own courses dedicated to some specific AI area. In addition, I will present you a structured way to teach everything around Copilots and Agents within the Dynamics 365 product family as well as the Power Platform product family.
