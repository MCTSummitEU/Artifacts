# From MCT to TSP: Sharing our learnings from the road

**Speaker(s):** Amelia Hernández Osorio, Andre Thiemann — session on the [MCT EU Summit 2026](https://e.runevents.net/mct-eu-summit-2026) agenda.

## Abstract

The Training Services Partner (TSP) path is one of the biggest myths in the MCT program. Do you qualify? What does Microsoft actually expect? And is it even worth it for trainers?
 
In this practical session, Amelia Hernández and Andre Thiemann cut through the noise and share their experiences of what it takes to go from MCT to TSP. 
 
You’ll get:

- Real stories from our personal journeys (what worked, what didn’t)
- The step‑by‑step process and realistic timelines
- Life after TSP status: what changes, what doesn’t, and how to activate opportunities

And the cherry on top: experiences from other TSPs in the MCT community, offering additional perspectives of the TSP journey. 
 
Walk away with a reusable checklist to become TSP you can apply the same day.
